Maja means home

Claudia, Martina and Federica, with no money but with a lot of energy, after a trip in India decided that the name of their incubator was Maja, that in cebuan means "Home".
Maja is not only a start-up incubator, it's a big family that connects people from allover the world with innovative ideas and projects that aim to create a positive and collective impact. We strongly believe in Our Values
